The three MIT-themed clues (1, 5, and 15) each have exactly two possible answers that fit the given space:

  • 1. MIT undergrad major (12 letters): ARCHITECTURE & ANTHROPOLOGY
  • 5. Overall direction of travel if you take the Red Line for 5 stops from Kendall/MIT (9 letters): NORTHWEST & SOUTHEAST (to Alewife and Broadway, respectively)
  • 15. MIT dorm (11 letters): SENIOR HOUSE & SIMMONS HALL

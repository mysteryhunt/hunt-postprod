Each of the people or fictional characters depicted has a first or last name (not both) that is a homophone, or a near-homophone, of a unit of measurement.

  • punky BREWSTER
  • GRAEME base
  • MAXWELL smart
  • MEGAWATI sukarnoputri
  • oliver STONE
  • doris DAY
  • ROD serling
  • lieutenant COLUMBO
  • mr. DARCY
  • samuel FOOTE
  • HENRY james
  • ezra POUND
  • JEWEL

They are arranged into the formula:

17/16 (brewster gram maxwell megawatt stone)² day rod / (coulomb darcy foot henry pound)² joule

which is approximately equal to 1 POISE.

--- layout: phantom_of_the_operator_solution title: Set Theory class: puzzle solution credits: by Michael Colao --- The object is to:
  1. determine what the two elements of each triangle have in common
  2. determine another valid member of the set to take the third leg of the equilateral triangle and
  3. do so in such a way that the three elements on the inner triangle sides also have something in common, that thing is the answer to each triangle puzzle.

Midnight, Chill, High Life, all are MILLER products

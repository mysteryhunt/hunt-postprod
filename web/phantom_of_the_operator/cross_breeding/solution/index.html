Solvers need to reconstruct the alphabetic list of bigrams and trigrams into various AKC dog breeds, then place them into the boxes so they intersect at a common letter. The crossbreeds of each intersecting pair must then be placed on a diagonal so they cross both pure breeds, as shown. The shaded squares reveal the answer (CLINTON) from top to bottom.

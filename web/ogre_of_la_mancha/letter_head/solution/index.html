The left column of clues is missing its first letters:

  • (M)Ike with a connection to Cleveland - HENRY
  • (O)Connor’s maiden name - DAY
  • (B)Read, in Rouen - PAIN
  • (Y)Awl, e.g. - BOAT
  • (D)Evils or Magic - TEAM
  • (I)Mage with a staff - NOTE (as in a musical note)
  • (C)Ode ending? - WORD
  • (K)Eel follower, at sea - HAUL
  • (F)Red on the green, at times - FUNK (as in the golf player)
  • (O)Men - SIGN
  • (R)After - BEAM
  • (O)Range part - SECTION
  • (N)Umber that colors opinions - RATING
  • (E)Lite, e.g. - TYPE (as in the printing term “elite type”)

The missing letters spell MOBY DICK, FOR ONE, which is a clue for BOOK (or is it NOVEL or WHALE?).

The right column of clues are categories, each of which can be filled by adding a letter to an answer from the first group:

  • V-SIGN
  • I-BEAM
  • R-RATING
  • T-NOTE
  • U-BOAT
  • A-TEAM
  • L WORD
  • P-FUNK
  • R-TYPE
  • O. HENRY
  • D-DAY
  • U-HAUL
  • C-SECTION
  • T-PAIN

The added letters spell VIRTUAL PRODUCT. This, taken with BOOK, clues the final answer, EBOOK.

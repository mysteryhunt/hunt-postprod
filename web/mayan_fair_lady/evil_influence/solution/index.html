First, solvers fill in the straightforward cryptic crossword (see below for full solution).

Solvers should then notice that there is a one-to-one correspondence between the letter O in the grid and the black and white circles to the left of the grid. When the Os are colored black and white, the grid doubles as a Masyu puzzle (clued by the title, which is the literal English translation for “Masyu”). After solving the Masyu, a final message can be read by starting at the top left corner and reading the non-O corners of the path as one moves clockwise: THE FINAL ANSWER IS DRUNK TANK.

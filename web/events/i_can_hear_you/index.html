--- layout: events title: I Can Hear You ---

Date: Saturday, January 14th, 2012 at 10:00am

Location: Lobdell Dining Hall, on the second floor of the MIT Student Center.

Have 2-4 team members who can communicate well join us at the Lobdell Dining Hall. During this event you will have 2 active team members; any additional ones are only available to swap in / out during breaks. Teams must have 2 battery operated, wireless internet connected devices (laptop, tablet, smart phone).

Please check your device for compatibility by visiting this page.

You may bring as many of the following as you’d like:

  • Paper
  • Pencils/pens
  • Markers
  • Flags
  • Non-wireless capable flashlights
  • String
  • Scissors
  • Binoculars
  • Tape

Please make sure to label anything that you wish to get back. Do not bring anything particularly fragile or valuable.

The title makes people think they’ll need Bway knowledge, but no: they get snippets of TV theme songs, instead. Teams will be instructed to bring at least one smartphone or laptop. They have to ID the show, and index into the show’s title by the number of notes given (which was the main thing used in the original “Name That Tune” game show). Each round spells out a TV clue, and the first/last letters of the clue answers taken in order spell the final answer TU/BE/TE/ST, TUBETEST.

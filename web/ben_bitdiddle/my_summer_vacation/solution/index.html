Read paragraphs! Each paragraph has clues to a red line station, a fictional station, and something to count. After realizing these things, one would travel to said red line stations and count said thing. The resulting number is then indexed into the name of the fiction station, ordered by position on the Red Line. (The original ordering is alphabetical by fictional station.) This leads to the solution MECHANICS.

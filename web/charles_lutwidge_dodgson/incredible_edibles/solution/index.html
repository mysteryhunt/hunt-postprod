The puzzle is a series of pictures. Each picture is a number made out of food. The foods are the ingredients (minus chocolate) of chocolate bars from Vosges. Index into the name of the chocolate bar by the depicted number. It will spell out CALL TO ASK FOR DESSERT. When solvers do, they are given a piece of chocolate (made by Alex Calhoun) with the real answer printed on it.
